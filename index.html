Why is Olivie one of my favorite authors?

Alexene Farol Follmuth, also known under the pen name Olivie Blake, is a lover and writer of stories, many involving fantasy, the paranormal, or the supernatural.All genres I'm obsessed with, being the bookworm that I am.

Furthermore, she explores in her books very interesting ideas like - what it means to be human (or not); the endlessly interesting complexities of life and love. She’s won the title of New York Times and Sunday Times bestselling author with The Atlas Six, which doesn't surprise me. Her writing transports you so deeply into the story that it’s truly impossible to put any of her books down!
